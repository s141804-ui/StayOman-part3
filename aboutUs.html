Who We Are

StayOman is a local platform that helps people find and explore rest houses across Oman. Our goal is to make it easy for users to discover, view, and contact rest house owners directly. We support local tourism by connecting travelers with small business owners and promoting Omani hospitality through simple, clear, and user-friendly design.
